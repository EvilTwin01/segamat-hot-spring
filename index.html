Labis Hot Spring is your family park of choice. Located in Labis, at the northern end of Segamat district.


Our all-season accommodation has a tranquil, park setting with magnificent mature puriri trees and an abundance of native birds. Great memories are created and a relaxing holiday for the whole family is guaranteed!

Accomodation

The best way to experience Hot Springs is with family and friends. The vacation rental houses are located near and have all the conveniences of home, the amazing scenery and atmosphere of our small town, and plenty of room to share with family or friends.

Playground

The little ones will enjoy tiring themselves out on the slides and jungle gyms on our playground! There is also a children’s pool within the Labis Hot Springs’ hot pools area for them to enjoy. So, everyone can enjoy and have fun!

Springs

Relax and unwind at Labis Hot Springs – thermal mineral pools heated to between 37 and 39 degrees Celsius. The beautifully soft water comes direct from nature and our hot pools are free for all Labis Hot Spring guests to use as much as they like!
