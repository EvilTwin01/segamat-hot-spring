Things To Know
Before You Come

All reservations require full deposit. At the time of reservation, we require a credit card number and full deposit will be processed. If you do not provide your credit card number when booking online, your "pending reservation" will be deleted.


If for any reason you need to cancel or reschedule your reservation, we require a 14-day notice prior. If we do not receive this notice, your reservation will NOT be refunded or moved. If you do give us a 14-day notice you will be refunded all of your paid deposit except a processing fee.

For reservations with a deposit of RM100 or less, there is a RM10 non-refundable processing fee that will be taken. For reservations with a deposit of more than RM100, there is a RM35 non-refundable processing fee that will be taken.


We require a 14-day notice to reschedule any reservation. Reservations can only be moved 1 time. There is a RM10 rescheduling fee that will apply to all reservations if you choose to reschedule within our guidelines.
