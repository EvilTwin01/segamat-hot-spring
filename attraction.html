Attraction

 

Let yourself go at
Labis Hot Springs

Labis hot Spring is one of the most popular hotspots in Johor. The place pulls a lot of crowd throughout the year. People visit the place to relax and heal themselves by taking a warm and soothing dip in the hot spring waters. The hot spring is located at a distance of 11 kilometers from the Labis town.


The government has protected the hot spring along with its watershed so as to offer the visitors and travelers the opportunity to engage themselves in a number of leisure activities which include the likes of picnicking.


Entrance and Spacious Carpark

The entrance invites people with all grace, however simple the setting is. There is a car parking space which runs over a huge area. There are trees planted at distance to keep the area cool and soothing.


Well-Structured Pool

The pool is very well structured. It is quite large and is divided into 4 pools. The 2 pools that are facing the men’s bathroom are not in operation. The functional pools are the 2 facing the women’s bathroom. Water from the springs falls into the pools of different depth. The deeper pool has higher water temperature while the shallow one has lower water temperature of about 40 degree celcius. You can also find a number of seating areas.


Exercise Equipment

A piece of adjacent land has been taken up and has been turned into a small park that has exercise equipment and two slides for the children to have some fun.


Garden Landscape

There is a man-made garden pool adjacent to the hot spring, which allow the visitors to take a walk around the greens. Not much beautification has been done and yet the area retains its own charm. There are a few relaxing pavilions offered for the visitors too.


Labis Hot Spring is a unique and remarkable corner of the state and we want to share it with visitors who are looking to experience a powerful sense of place created by nature. There’s only one hot springs in Segamat and it’s right here in Labis Hot Spring!
